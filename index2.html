My INFSCI 3005 Blog - Fall 2017

Blog I: Big Data Colloquium Friday, 29 September 2017

Discovering Communities and Anomalies in Attributed Graphs: Interactive Visual Exploration and Summarization

Dr. Leman Akoglu

I arrived on Friday to very packed talk by Dr. Akoglu full of eager minds and obstructed views to hear her discussion on anomalies in attributed graphs. From where I was sitting (in what can be considered the nosebleed seats), she was just beginning her explanation of calculating optimization of normality. Unfortunately for me, there was little context to her words as I could not see the screen, but the thought of optimizing normality interested me. The discussion then flowed in to the graphs and data she used and how randomizing nodes within a given group could be utilized in order to identify attributes and see the anomalies within social networks like FaceBook. The research she presented is along the line of her dissertation which focuses on mining and modeling real world networks.
